## Frequently Asked Questions

**How many sensors do I need for my warehouse?**

Typically 1-2 sensors per refrigeration/freezer unit, plus sensors for loading docks, staging areas, and critical storage zones. We provide a free facility assessment to recommend optimal coverage.

**What's the range of the sensors?**

Sensors communicate with gateways up to 300 feet (line of sight). For large warehouses, we deploy multiple gateways to ensure complete coverage.

**Can Cool Track monitor refrigerated trucks?**

Yes. GPS-enabled sensors track temperature during transport. They log data offline if connectivity is lost, then upload when connection resumes.

**How long do sensor batteries last?**

3-5 years depending on reporting frequency. Low battery alerts notify you well in advance of replacement needs.

**Is Cool Track compatible with our existing WMS?**

Yes. Our REST API integrates with most WMS, ERP, and facility management systems. We also provide direct data export options.

**What happens during a power outage?**

Sensors continue logging on battery backup. The system automatically reconnects when power returns and syncs all missed data.

**Do we need IT support to install Cool Track?**

No. Sensors are plug-and-play with automatic discovery. Basic internet connectivity is all that's required. Our support team assists with initial setup.

**Can we try Cool Track before committing?**

Yes. Contact us to discuss evaluation options for your facility.
