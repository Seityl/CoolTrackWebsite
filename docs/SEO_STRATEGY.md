### Content Optimization

#### Word Count Targets
- Homepage: 800-1,200 words
- Feature pages: 600-900 words
- Industry pages: 1,000-1,500 words
- Blog posts: 1,500-2,500 words

#### Keyword Density
- Primary keyword: 1-2% density
- Secondary keywords: 0.5-1% density
- Natural language, avoid keyword stuffing
- Use variations and synonyms

#### Content Best Practices
1. Answer user intent immediately (first 100 words)
2. Use bullet points for scannability
3. Include data and statistics
4. Add trust signals (testimonials, certifications)
5. Use active voice
6. Keep sentences under 20 words
7. Paragraphs under 150 words

## Technical SEO Requirements

### Performance Targets
- Page load time: < 2 seconds
- First Contentful Paint: < 1.5 seconds
- Largest Contentful Paint: < 2.5 seconds
- Cumulative Layout Shift: < 0.1

### Mobile Optimization
- 100% mobile responsive
- Touch targets min 48x48px
- No horizontal scrolling
- Mobile-first indexing optimized
